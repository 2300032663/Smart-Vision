Project Title

GigShield – AI-Powered Income Protection for Gig Workers

1. Problem Statement
 Gig workers such as delivery partners working with platforms like Swiggy, Zomato, Amazon, and Zepto depend on daily deliveries to earn income. However, external   disruptions such as heavy rain, floods, pollution, traffic restrictions, or curfews can prevent them from working and reduce their earnings.
 Currently, gig workers have no insurance protection for income loss caused by these uncontrollable events. When disruptions occur, workers lose a portion of       their income with no financial support.Our project GigShield aims to solve this problem by providing an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that              automatically compensates workers for income lost due to external disruptions.

2. Objective of the Solution
 The goal of this project is to create a digital platform that protects gig workers’ income by:
 Detecting external disruptions such as extreme weather or pollution
 Automatically estimating the worker’s income loss
 Triggering insurance claims automatically
 Providing instant payouts to workers
 The platform focuses only on income protection and does not include accident, health, or vehicle repair insurance.

3. Target Users (Persona)
 Our primary users are delivery partners working in the gig economy, including:
 Food delivery workers (Swiggy, Zomato)
 E-commerce delivery partners (Amazon, Flipkart)
 Quick commerce delivery workers (Blinkit, Zepto)
 These workers typically depend on daily or weekly earnings, making them highly vulnerable to sudden disruptions.

4. Key Features of the Application
 A. Worker Onboarding
 Workers can register on the platform by providing basic information such as:
 Name and phone number
 Delivery platform (Swiggy, Zomato, etc.)
 City and delivery zone
 Vehicle type
 Weekly earnings
 This information helps calculate risk and determine the insurance premium.
 B. AI-Powered Risk Assessment
 The system analyzes multiple external factors such as:
 Weather conditions
 Flood-prone zones
 Air pollution levels
 Traffic restrictions
 Using this data, the AI model calculates a risk score and suggests an appropriate weekly insurance premium.
 C. Weekly Insurance Plans
 The insurance model is designed around a weekly premium structure, which matches the earning cycle of gig workers.
 Example plans:
 Basic Plan – ₹20 per week (Coverage up to ₹2000)
 Standard Plan – ₹25 per week (Coverage up to ₹3000)
 Premium Plan – ₹35 per week (Coverage up to ₹5000)
 Workers can select a plan based on their income level.
 D. Disruption Detection
 The system continuously monitors external data sources such as:
 Weather APIs
 Traffic alerts
 Local emergency notifications
 When a disruption occurs in the worker’s delivery zone, the system detects the event automatically.
 E. Automatic Claim Trigger
 Once a disruption is verified, the platform automatically:
 Detects reduced delivery activity
 Estimates the worker’s lost working hours
 Calculates the income loss
 A claim is triggered without requiring manual submission from the worker.
 F. Instant Payout System
 After claim verification, the worker receives a payout instantly through digital payment methods such as:
 UPI
 Bank transfer
 Digital wallet
 The worker can also download a receipt for the payout from the dashboard.
 G. Fraud Detection
 To prevent misuse of the system, AI-based fraud detection mechanisms are used, including:
 Location verification using GPS
 Weather event validation
 Detection of duplicate claims
 Anomaly detection in claim patterns
 H. Dashboard and Analytics
 Worker Dashboard
 Workers can view:
 Active insurance coverage
 Weekly premium details
 Disruption alerts
 Claim history
 Downloadable payout receipts
 Admin Dashboard
 Insurance providers can view:
 Total number of users
 Claim frequency
 Disruption patterns
 Predicted risks for upcoming weeks

5. Application Workflow
 The typical workflow of the platform is as follows:
 Worker registers on the application
 Worker enters profile details and delivery zone
 AI calculates risk score and suggests weekly premium
 Worker purchases an insurance plan
 System monitors external disruptions
 Disruption occurs (e.g., heavy rain)
 AI estimates income loss and triggers claim
 Instant payout is sent to the worker

By combining AI-powered risk   assessment, parametric insurance triggers, and instant payouts, the platform ensures financial stability for workers who rely on daily deliveries for their        livelihood.
